-  <section title="Tiles">
-    <p>
-      Tiles in Windstille are 128x128 pixel large, there collision
-      handling however happens at 16x16 pixels. This means that a tile
-      is seperated into 8x8 subtiles.
-    </p>
-    
-    <p align="center">
-      <img src="images/8x8.jpg" />
-      <img src="images/8x8-1.jpg" />
-    </p>
-
-    <p>
-      Which of these subtiles is collidable and which isn't is setup
-      in the <tt>data/tiles.scm</tt> file. So to create a tile one
-      needs to paint a 128x128 large tile and make sure that any edges
-      of it align with the 16x16 subtiles so that collision handling
-      can go smoothly. A little overlap is ok and necessary to make it
-      look good, but the player shouldn't be allowed to 'walk in
-      stone', so overlap shouldn't get to huge. The yellow areas above
-      mark transition-subtiles, aka. tiles that are only half
-      collidable and present a 45 degree angle.
-    </p>
